A Cockapoo


Fact #1: Cockapoos are Low-Allergen and Low-Shedding - The amount of hair a normal Cockapoo sheds is extremely minimal.


Fact #2: Cockapoos Have A Longer Life Span Than Most Dog Breeds.


Fact #3: Cockapoos Are Highly Intelligent.


Fact #4: Cockapoos are always full of energy! They love fetch!


Fact #5: Cockapoos are very friendly to children and other animals - they are always want to cuddle.


Fact #6: Cockapoos love meeting new people or animals!


Fact #7: Cockapoos don't smell like your average dog.


Fact #8: Cockapoos are one of the oldest known designer dog breeds.


Fact #9: Cockapoos are also used as therapy dogs.


Fact #10: Some Cockapoos are trained to be hearing dogs for the deaf.
